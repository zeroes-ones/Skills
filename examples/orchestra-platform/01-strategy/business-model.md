# Orchestra Platform — Business Model

## Business Model Canvas

| Block | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Customer Segments** | Engineering leaders (VP/CTO) at 50–200 dev orgs; platform engineering leads |
| **Value Proposition** | Reduce infrastructure toil from 30% to <5%; go live in hours, not months |
| **Channels** | Content marketing (blogs, templates, webinars), developer conferences, outbound sales |
| **Customer Relationships** | Self-serve onboarding, Slack community, dedicated CSM at Pro tier |
| **Revenue Streams** | Per-seat subscriptions + platform fee + plugin marketplace commission (20%) |
| **Key Resources** | Engineering team, open-source Backstage expertise, plugin ecosystem |
| **Key Activities** | Product development, plugin marketplace curation, community growth |
| **Key Partners** | Cloud providers (AWS), plugin developers, SI/reseller partners |
| **Cost Structure** | Engineering salaries (70%), cloud infrastructure (15%), GTM (10%), G&A (5%) |

## Unit Economics

- **CAC:** $800 (blend of content marketing, developer conference sponsorships, and a lean inside-sales team)
- **LTV:** $14,400 (average customer stays 24 months at $29/dev/month × 20-seat average = $580/month, plus $500 platform fee = $1,080/month × 24 months, discounted for churn)
- **LTV:CAC Ratio:** 18:1 — well above the 3:1 benchmark for healthy SaaS, driven by low-touch onboarding and strong retention

## TAM / SAM / SOM

- **TAM:** $2.4B (8,000 companies × average $300K annual contract value)
- **SAM:** $600M (English-speaking markets, cloud-native orgs, excluding heavily regulated industries)
- **SOM:** $30M by Year 3 (capturing approximately 5% of SAM through direct sales and self-serve)

## Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Starter** | $29/dev/month | Service catalog, 5 templates, community plugins, email support |
| **Pro** | $49/dev/month | Unlimited templates, plugin marketplace access, Slack support, analytics |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | SSO/SAML, SLA (99.9%), dedicated CSM, audit logs, on-prem option |

## Revenue Projections

| Year | ARR | Customers | Assumptions |
|------|-----|-----------|-------------|
| Year 1 | $360K | 30 | 15% monthly growth, starting from 5 design partners |
| Year 2 | $2.4M | 120 | Expansion revenue from Pro upgrades, plugin marketplace launch |
| Year 3 | $12M | 400 | Series A funding accelerates GTM, SI partner channel opens |

## Key Assumptions

- **70% annual net revenue retention** (measured quarterly)
- **15% month-over-month customer growth in Year 1**, tapering to 8% by Year 3
- **Average seat count: 20** in Year 1, growing to 35 by Year 3 as orgs expand usage
- **Gross margin: 80%** (cloud infrastructure remains predictable at scale)

## What "Good" Looks Like

This model was built bottom-up from unit assumptions rather than top-down from market size. Every number was pressure-tested through 15 prospect interviews. A healthy business looks like: LTV:CAC above 5:1, payback period under 12 months, and net revenue retention above 100% (indicating expansion within existing accounts).
