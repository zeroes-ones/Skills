# Term Challenging

Active interrogation of every domain term to ensure precision. Never accept a term at face value — ask "What exactly does this mean here?"

## The Challenging Protocol
1. **Identify** the term in context (code, conversation, doc)
2. **Question** its boundaries: "Does X include Y? Does it exclude Z?"
3. **Test** with edge cases: "What happens when..."
4. **Record** the refined definition in CONTEXT.md

## Example Challenges
- "User" → "Is this an account holder, an authenticated session, or a person identity?"
- "Order" → "Does it include items still in cart? What about canceled orders?"
- "Active" → "Active as of when? Active for billing, for login, or for support?"

## Signs a Term Needs Challenging
- Two team members give different definitions
- The term appears in multiple contexts with slightly different behavior
- Business stakeholders use a different word than developers
