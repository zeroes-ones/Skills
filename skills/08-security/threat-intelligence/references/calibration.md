# Calibration — How to Know Your Level

| Level | You Know You're At This Level When... |
|---|---|
| **L1 (Novice)** | You can consume IOC feeds, run basic threat hunts using provided queries, and map an incident to MITRE ATT&CK techniques using the navigator. |
| **L2 (Competent)** | You independently profile threat actors, create STIX 2.1 intelligence reports, run hypothesis-driven hunts, and operate a MISP/OpenCTI instance. |
| **L3 (Proficient)** | You design the organization's intelligence collection plan, define PIRs, build automated intelligence pipelines (ingestion → enrichment → dissemination), and brief executives on the threat landscape. |
| **L4 (Expert)** | You conduct original threat research — reverse-engineer malware C2 protocols, discover novel threat actor infrastructure, publish intelligence to ISAC communities, and mentor junior analysts. |
| **L5 (Master)** | You contribute to threat intelligence standards (STIX, TAXII), publish named threat actor research cited by government agencies, and design national-level intelligence sharing frameworks. |
