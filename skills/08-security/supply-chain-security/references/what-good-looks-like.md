# What Good Looks Like — Full Quality Standard

Every release has a verifiable SLSA Level 3 provenance attestation generated by an isolated, hermetic build environment. An SBOM in CycloneDX 1.6 format with VEX integration accompanies every artifact, signed and published to a transparency log. All dependencies are scanned at full transitive depth with automated Dependabot/Renovate PRs merging critical CVE patches within 24 hours. CI/CD pipelines use OIDC federation exclusively — no long-lived credentials exist anywhere in the build infrastructure. Vendor risk assessments are continuous, not point-in-time, with automated SBOM collection and vulnerability monitoring. Artifact signatures are verified at every deployment gate via policy-as-code admission control. The entire supply chain from signed commit to production deployment is cryptographically verifiable in under 30 seconds.
