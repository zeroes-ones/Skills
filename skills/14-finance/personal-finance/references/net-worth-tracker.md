## Key Ratios

| Ratio | Formula | Current | Target |
|-------|---------|---------|--------|
| Savings Rate | (Annual Savings / Gross Income) x 100 | ___% | 15-25% |
| Emergency Fund | (Liquid Savings / Monthly Expenses) | ___ months | 3-6 months |
| Debt-to-Income | (Monthly Debt / Monthly Gross Income) x 100 | ___% | <36% |
| Net Worth to Income | Net Worth / Annual Gross Income | ___x | See benchmarks |
| Investment Fees | Weighted avg expense ratio | ___% | <0.15% |
