# Spec Handoff Protocol: Brainstorming → Implementation

## Artifact Requirements
When handing off from brainstorming to implementation, the design brief must include:

1. **Problem Statement:** One sentence with evidence.
2. **Success Criteria:** Measurable metrics with baseline and target.
3. **Persona:** Specific user with current behavior and pain point.
4. **MVP Scope:** Single deliverable for first iteration.
5. **Constraint Inventory:** All constraints with origin stories.
6. **Approaches Considered:** At least 3 with documented rationale for rejection.
7. **Trade-Off Matrix:** Gains and losses for chosen approach.
8. **Uncertainty Inventory:** Known-unknowns with resolution plans.
9. **Decision Traceability:** Why this design was chosen over alternatives.

## Acceptance Criteria Format
Each requirement in the design brief must translate to at least one testable acceptance criterion:
- "Given [context], when [action], then [observable outcome]."

## Coordination Checklist
- [ ] Design brief reviewed and approved
- [ ] Implementation skill identified (fullstack-developer, backend-developer, frontend-developer)
- [ ] Uncertainty resolution plan assigned to owner
- [ ] Chesterton's Fence audit complete — no unexplained constraint removals
- [ ] Rollback plan documented for architectural decisions
