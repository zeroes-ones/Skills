# Shared Understanding Confirmation Protocol

## The Restatement Test
After the decision tree is complete, ask the user:
"Based on everything we've walked through, restate the decision and its rationale in your own words."

### Pass Criteria
- The restatement captures the root decision accurately.
- The rationale matches the documented branch resolutions.
- The user can explain WHY alternatives were rejected.

### Divergence Detection
If the restatement diverges from the documented tree:
- Identify the specific branch where understanding diverged.
- Re-grill that branch. The resolution was premature or misunderstood.
- Do not override the user's understanding with the documented version — the documented version may be wrong.

## Multi-Stakeholder Confirmation
When multiple people were grilled (or decisions affect multiple stakeholders):
- Each person restates in their own words independently.
- Compare restatements. Divergence between people = the decision was not truly shared.
- Reconcile divergences before declaring completion.
