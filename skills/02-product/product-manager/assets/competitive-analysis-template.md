---

- [Scenario 2]
- [Scenario 3]

- [Scenario 1 — e.g., "When the buyer values speed of implementation over feature breadth"]

| They Say | We Respond | Proof Point |

## References

- _Playing to Win_ by A.G. Lafley & Roger L. Martin — for strategy and where-to-play choices
- _Good Strategy, Bad Strategy_ by Richard Rumelt — for strategic analysis fundamentals
- Porter's Five Forces — Michael Porter, _Competitive Strategy_
- Blue Ocean Strategy by W. Chan Kim & Renée Mauborgne — for finding uncontested market space
- G2 / Capterra / TrustRadius — review aggregation platforms
- Crunchbase / PitchBook — funding and company intelligence
- Google Alerts — free competitor monitoring
